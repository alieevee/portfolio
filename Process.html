What was I creating for?


In the modern era where digital portfolios are the new CVs, young jobseekers must keep up with this trend. It is not only a chance to show off some skills in plain HTML/CSS, but also makes life easier for the employer who has to handle many many documents lost in their stack of folders. For this task I have decided to create a modern block design portfolio, with just essential features so to not complicate the website. There were several components that I considered important:

  • Header stating my name
  • Menu that would pop out when clicked
  • An about section that linked to my CV
  • A contact section
...and so I started the next phase of the design process: UI

Sketching it


Here I used sketchable to roughly outline the UI. Following this step, I would also use Adobe Illustrator to further sophisticate the designs, however, I decided not to overcomplicate it this time.
